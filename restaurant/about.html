ABOUT THF

The Hazelnut Factory is a one-stop food dreamland for all. Mouthwatering desserts, innovative and flavoursome menu of sweets, specialty coffee, delectable food menu, eye- catching and spectacular packaging and exhilarating European interiors create a unique experience for anybody who enters our doors. Opened in 2019 in the city of Nawabs, THF is loved by all age groups. We follow best global practices in manufacturing our products and deal with state of the art production equipment and techniques. THF is the fastest growing F&B brand in Uttar Pradesh today waiting to open its arms in various parts of the country. We pride ourselves on providing a superior customer experience, from the moment you walk into our outlets to the last bite of your favourite treat. You'll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy our fresh, flavorful treats. Our friendly team is always on hand to help you choose the perfect pastry or sweet to satisfy your cravings. Also, we're committed to make every visit a delightful and memorable experience.So why wait? Come, visit us today and discover the ambiance where every visit is an escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

BEHIND THE SCENES (WHERE ALL THE MAGIC HAPPENS)

Welcome to the biggest bakery kitchen in Uttar Pradesh! At our state-of-the-art facility, we combine skilled workers, state-of-the-art machinery, and finely sourced ingredients to craft delectable sweets and baked goods. With a strong focus on quality, we strive to deliver the best products to our customers.


Our kitchen is staffed with highly skilled artisans who are passionate about their craft. Their expertise and dedication shine through in every product we create. Whether it's artisanal breads, fusion mithai, namkeens, or savoury items, our team ensures that each item is meticulously crafted to perfection.


To guarantee exceptional taste and quality, we prioritize the use of fresh ingredients. We understand that the key to great baked goods lies in the quality of the components. Therefore, we source our ingredients from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to excellence.

To maintain the highest standards, we have implemented a rigorous quality control process. From the moment the ingredients enter our kitchen to the final packaging of our products, every step is carefully monitored. We leave no room for compromise when it comes to the satisfaction of our customers.

Our ultimate aim is to create the best sweets and bakery products in India. We believe that our customers deserve nothing but the finest treats, and we pour our hearts into achieving that goal. Our dedication to excellence has earned us a reputation as the go-to bakery for those who appreciate quality and taste.


When you step into our store, you'll be greeted with a tempting array of treats. From flaky croissants to luscious cakes, from mouthwatering cookies to artisanal desserts & wide variety of mithai, we have something to satisfy every craving. We take pride in offering a wide variety of baked goods that cater to diverse tastes and preferences.

MEET OUR TEAM

ANKIT SAHNI (Founder, THF)

Our founder, Mr Ankit Sahni, has always been captivated by the idea of opening a cafe that combines the best of coffee, bakery products, and sweets under one roof. It was this burning passion that led him to embark on a remarkable journey in 2018. Recognizing the scarcity of places offering a diverse range of coffee, bakery treats, and sweets, Ankit decided to fill this gap by establishing The Hazelnut Factory. With unwavering determination, he dedicated an entire year to meticulously crafting the concept before finally unveiling the first THF store in 2019.

At THF, we take pride in being a vegetarian cafe with an enticing array of egg products in our bakery section. We also understand the importance of catering to different dietary preferences, which is why we offer a plethora of gluten-free and vegan options. Our menu predominantly features delectable continental dishes, while our beverage menu is equally extensive, ensuring there's something for every discerning palate.

What truly sets THF apart is our commitment to excellence in every bite and sip. We source specialty coffees from the finest plantations in Chingmanglur, ensuring an exceptional coffee experience for our customers. Our expert bakers work tirelessly to produce freshly baked croissants, craft exquisite sandwiches, and create mouthwatering desserts that leave a lasting impression. Additionally, we take pride in our artisanal bread selection, featuring classics like sourdough, baguettes, bagels, and more.

But the indulgence doesn't end there. THF is not just a cafe and bakery; it is also a haven for handcrafted artisanal sweets. Our skilled confectioners put their heart and soul into infusing unique flavors into our sweets, ranging from tantalizing blueberry and cranberry creations to irresistible favorites like tiramisu and Lotus Biscoff. At THF, we strive to offer a culinary adventure that surpasses your wildest imagination. With over 70 varieties of artisanal sweets and baklava, every visit to THF is a discovery of extraordinary flavors.

The success of THF speaks volumes about our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction. Within a span of three years, THF has experienced exponential growth, with revenues skyrocketing by more than 50 times. Each month, we have the privilege of hosting approximately 150,000 individuals who trust us to deliver exceptional taste and an unparalleled dining experience.
